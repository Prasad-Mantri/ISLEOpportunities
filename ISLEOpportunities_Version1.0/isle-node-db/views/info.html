What is an opportunity?

An opportunity describes a specific role in an activity, program, project, or event. Therefore, a separate entry must be created for each student role within your activity, program, project, or event.

To create an opportunity, complete the form below. You may SAVE your work and return to it later as needed. When you have completed the form, click the SUBMIT FOR APPROVAL or PUBLISH button. All published opportunities are visible to students in the ISLE opportunity catalog. You may manage your opportunities through the ISLE Opportunity Manager.

The information you enter will inform the system about when to display your opportunity in the catalog. This information will also be processed by the recommendation engine to serve up appropriate opportunities for students based on their current growth model as well as major, academic standing, co-op status, and more.
